What is a Project?

a project is a series of tasks that need to be completed in order to reach a specific outcome. A project can also be defined as a set of inputs and outputs required to achieve a particular goal. An information technology project includes all aspects of planning, design, implementation, project management, and training related to the endeavor.

Step 1

know your title

  • Build a Tribute page

Step 2

Know your goal

After sorting out my title. Then I figure out my goal. my goal is for the title of my project to come to pass as i want it to be done.

Step 3

Documentation of the project

write and screenshot

Date and time is very inportant to me. I want to know how fast I am and how I manage my time for any project. Documentation helps me alot. If I am building another project. The saved work or code style can be re-use.

Check if you have your goal

I asked myself, did I have what I have planned for? or did I meet my goal? What was my fault if I dont meet my goals? I have to do more checking on whether my goals are met. Do not wait for people to give you projects. Just think about what you want to create or build and just work on that.Remember to set your goal
